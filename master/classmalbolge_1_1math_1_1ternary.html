Detailed Description

Ternary unsigned integer type.

Malbolge has a single type: a 10 digit ternary (base3) unsigned integer.

Underlying binary integer type.

It is wide enough to hold two lots of the maximum value a 3^10 ternary type can represent, this is to allow the summation (and subsequent modulo) of two Malbolge ternary values.
